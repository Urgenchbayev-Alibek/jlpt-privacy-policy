JLPT Master — Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 1, 2026

Introduction: This Privacy Policy describes how JLPT Master mobile application and website ("Platform") collects, uses, and protects personal information of its users. We are committed to maintaining the privacy and security of user data in accordance with applicable laws and regulations.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information from our users:

  • Personal Identification Information: Full name, phone number (used for authentication via SMS verification), and unique user ID assigned by our system.
  • Learning Progress Data: Lesson viewing history and completion percentage, test scores and performance metrics, learning progress tracking, answers to practice questions, achievement records, and completion of course milestones.
  • Device & Technical Information: Device type and model (iOS/Android), operating system version, IP address, device identifiers, app version information, and access timestamps for security and device binding purposes (enforcing "1 Account = 1 Device" policy).
  • Media Files: Camera and gallery permissions are requested to allow users to upload profile pictures or other necessary media for their learning profile.
  • Usage Analytics: Session duration, feature usage patterns, navigation behavior within the app, and crash reports for performance improvement.

2. How We Use Your Information

The collected information is used for the following purposes:

  • Service Provision: Enabling access to course content, providing video lectures, managing learning progress, delivering test assessments, and supporting interactive features.
  • Samur.ai Analysis: Our AI-powered system analyzes your test responses and learning patterns to provide personalized feedback, recommendations for improvement, and tailored learning paths based on your performance.
  • Certification: Generating QR-coded certificates for users who successfully complete courses, including user verification and certificate validation.
  • Push Notifications: Sending notifications about subscription expiration, course updates, learning reminders, and important platform announcements.
  • Account Security: Enforcing the "1 Account = 1 Device" policy to prevent unauthorized access and maintain subscription terms.
  • Service Improvement: Analyzing usage patterns and performance data to enhance user experience, fix bugs, and develop new features.

3. Security & Content Protection (Anti-Piracy Measures)

The Platform implements comprehensive security measures to protect intellectual property and prevent unauthorized distribution:

  • Device Binding: Each subscription is tied to a single device. Logging in from a new device will automatically log out the previous device, preventing account sharing.
  • Screenshot & Screen Recording Protection: The mobile app prevents users from taking screenshots or recording videos during lesson playback. Attempting to bypass these protections violates our Terms of Service.
  • Content Streaming Only: Video content is delivered through secure streaming only. Users cannot download videos to their devices for offline viewing.
  • Watermarking: On the web platform, video playback includes a visible watermark displaying the user's phone number/ID to discourage unauthorized sharing.
  • DRM & Encryption: Content delivery utilizes industry-standard encryption and digital rights management technologies.
  • Account Monitoring: We monitor for suspicious activity and suspicious usage patterns that may indicate account compromise or content theft.

4. Data Storage & Third-Party Sharing

  • Secure Storage: All user data is stored on secure, encrypted servers with industry-standard security protocols.
  • Data Protection: We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect personal data against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.
  • No Selling of Data: We do NOT sell, rent, or share personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.
  • Third-Party Services: Data is only shared with trusted service providers who assist us in operating the Platform (e.g., payment processors, cloud hosting services, SMS verification services). These partners are contractually obligated to maintain confidentiality.
  • AI Processing: Learning performance data is processed by our Samur.ai system for analysis and personalized recommendations. This processing occurs within our secure infrastructure.
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose information when required by law or to protect our legal rights, user safety, or the Platform's security.
  • Data Retention: We retain user data for as long as the account is active and for a reasonable period afterward to comply with legal obligations. Deleted accounts have their data securely erased according to our data retention policy.

5. User Responsibilities & Code of Conduct

  • Users are responsible for maintaining the confidentiality of their login credentials and account information.
  • Sharing account credentials with others is strictly prohibited.
  • Users agree to comply with all applicable laws and the Platform's Terms of Service.
  • Unauthorized reproduction, distribution, or modification of Platform content is prohibited.
  • Any attempt to bypass security measures, including device binding, screenshot protection, or screen recording locks, constitutes a violation of our Terms of Service.
  • Users are responsible for any activities conducted under their account.

6. Children's Privacy

JLPT Master is designed for users aged 13 and above. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that a child under 13 has provided information, we will take steps to delete such information and terminate the child's account. Parents or guardians who believe their child has provided information should contact us immediately.

7. Your Privacy Rights

  • Access: You have the right to request and access the personal information we hold about you.
  • Correction: You can request correction of inaccurate or incomplete information.
  • Deletion: You have the right to request deletion of your account and associated personal data, subject to legal retention requirements.
  • Data Portability: Upon request, we can provide your data in a portable format.
  • Opt-Out: You can opt out of non-essential notifications by adjusting app settings or contacting support.

8. Cookies & Tracking Technologies

Our web platform may use cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance user experience, remember preferences, and analyze usage. Users can control cookie settings through their browser preferences. Note that disabling cookies may limit access to certain Platform features.

9. Payment & Subscription Information

  • We comply with PCI DSS (Payment Card Industry Data Security Standard) requirements.
  • Subscription duration is 6 months (180 days) from the purchase date.
  • Users receive notifications 7 days before subscription expiration.
  • Expired subscriptions result in automatic access revocation to course content.

  • No Refunds: Accounts terminated for policy violations are not eligible for refunds, regardless of remaining subscription period.
  • Legal Action: Serious violations may result in legal proceedings to recover damages in accordance with applicable laws.
